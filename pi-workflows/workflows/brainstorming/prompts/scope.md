Assess whether the user's idea is small enough for one design spec.

If it spans multiple independent subsystems, mark it as needing decomposition and identify the sub-projects, their dependencies, and the best first sub-project. Do not refine details of an oversized project before decomposing it.

Also decide whether upcoming questions are likely to benefit from visual treatment (mockups, layouts, architecture diagrams, side-by-side visual options). A UI topic is not automatically visual; offer visual help only when seeing something would beat reading text.
